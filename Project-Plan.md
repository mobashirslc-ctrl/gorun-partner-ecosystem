# GORUN - Hyper-Local Business & Logistics Ecosystem

**GORUN** holo ekta unified platform ja elakar merchant, qualified staff, rider ebong customer-der moddhe ekta "Hyper-Local Network" toiri kore. Er maddhome inventory sharing, on-demand skilled staffing, ebong fast delivery nishchit kora hoy.

---

## 🚀 Core Features

### 1. Merchant Dashboard (The Buyer/Seller)
Merchant-ra registration-er somoy nirdishto business category select korbe (Medicine, Grocery, Fashion, Corporate).
* **Fixed Category Hiring:** Merchant shudhu tar category-r sathe mil ache emon "Qualified Staff" hire korte parbe.
* **Category-Specific Inventory:** * *Medicine:* Expiry date, Generic name, Stock alerts.
    * *Grocery:* Weight (kg/gm), Unit, Brand.
    * *Fashion:* Size (S/M/L/XL), Color, Fabric.
* **M2M Sourcing (Shadow Inventory):** Pasher dokan theke emergency stock niye customer service deya.

### 2. Qualified Staff Dashboard (The Experts)
* **Skill-Based Profile:** Staff-ra tader expertise (e.g., Pharma, Fashion retail) onujayi verified tag pabe.
* **On-Demand Hourly Hiring:** 1-10 hours porjonto fixed hourly rate-e kaj korar sujog.
* **Digital ID Card:** App-er bhitore QR code thakbe authentication-er jonno.

### 3. Rider Dashboard (The Logistics Engine)
* **Live Task Feed:** M2M transfer, Home delivery, ebong Wholesale sourcing tasks.
* **OTP Verification:** Secure delivery nishchit korar jonno digital verification system.
* **Earnings Tracker:** Protidin-er income ebong task history tracking.

### 4. Customer Dashboard (The Consumer)
* **Local Market Browsing:** Elakar verified merchant-der stock dekha o order deya.
* **Instant Delivery:** GORUN rider-der maddhome druto home delivery pawa.

---

## 🛠️ Unique Selling Points (USP)

* **Fixed Category Matching:** Fashion shop shudhu fashion expert dekhbe, Corporate office shudhu corporate staff hire korte parbe.
* **Shadow Inventory:** Dokandar-ra tader obikrito ponno ba barti stock ek-oporer sathe share korbe. Ete choto dokaner sell barbe, ar boro dokander investment charai stock barbe.
* **On-Demand Staffing:** Permanently employee na rekhe proyojon onujayi "Skilled Staff" rent korar sujog.

---

## 📊 Revenue Model

1.  **Staff Commission:** Hourly payment theke 20-30% platform fee.
2.  **Delivery Charge:** Shop-to-shop o Shop-to-home delivery fee.
3.  **Wholesale Sourcing Fee:** Bazar theke mal anar jonno weight-based service charge.
4.  **M2M Transaction Fee:** Ek dokan theke onno dokane mal transfer-er jonno micro-fee.

## 💻 Technical Stack

* **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3 (Green-White Bright Theme), JavaScript.
* **Backend:** Firebase (Realtime Database, Authentication).
* **Architecture:** Modular and Scalable (PWA supported).

## 📂 Project Structure

- `/merchant/` - Login, Inventory, Hiring Logic.
- `/staff/` - Profile, Job Feed, Digital ID.
- `/rider/` - Delivery Tasks, OTP System.
- `/customer/` - Local Shop Browsing.
- `/assets/` - Logo, Branding, Global CSS.
